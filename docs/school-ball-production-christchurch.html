School Ball Production Christchurch

We handle the production. You handle the students.

Whether you need the full package or just a few extras to complete your vision, we've got you covered. From DJs and photo booths to lighting, theming, and everything in between. Available across New Zealand.

FAQ

How far in advance should we book?

As early as you can. Ball season is busy and we can only take on a limited number each year. Schools that book in Term 1 for a Term 2 or 3 ball have the best chance of getting their preferred date.

What's included in a school ball package?

It depends on what you need. A typical package includes a DJ with sound system and dance floor lighting, but you can add photo booths, glambots, LED screens, theming packages, uplighting, gobos, disco balls, archways, and more. We'll put together a custom quote based on your vision and budget.

Can we choose our own DJ?

Absolutely. Bring whoever you want. We'll provide the DJ gear (CDJ-3000s, mixer, booth monitor) and make sure they have everything they need. If you don't have a DJ lined up, we can sort one.

Do you do theming?

Yes. We have ready-to-go theming packages (Hollywood, Starry Night, Winter Wonderland, Enchanted Forest, and more) or you can mix and match individual items. Gobo lights, archways, LED trees, flower walls, starlight curtains, and uplighting in any colour.

Can you do afterparties too?

Definitely. We have inflatable party cubes, marquees, silent disco headsets, and portable sound and lighting for afterparty venues. Same crew, same night.

Do you work outside Christchurch?

Yes. We're based in Christchurch but available nationwide across New Zealand. We've done formals in venues all over Canterbury and beyond.
