School Ball Production Christchurch

We handle the production. You handle the students.

Planning a school ball is a lot of work. Booking the venue, sorting the theme, managing tickets, and then figuring out who's doing the sound and lighting on top of all that. We take the production side off your plate completely.

We've done balls at Rochester, Rutherford Hall, Tupuanuku, and plenty of other Christchurch venues. We know the power setups, the load-in access, and where to rig the truss without blocking sightlines. Our crew arrives in the afternoon, builds the show, runs it all night, and packs it out afterwards. You don't need to think about it.

What's Included

01.

Sound

DJ Booth

Full DJ setup with CDJ-3000s, DJM mixer, booth monitoring, and a proper truss booth or facade. Your DJ just needs to turn up with their USB sticks. We have the rest covered.

FAQ

How far in advance should we book?

As early as you can. Ball season is busy and we can only take on a limited number each year. Schools that book in Term 1 for a Term 2 or 3 ball have the best chance of getting their preferred date.

What's included in a school ball package?

A typical package includes PA and sound for speeches and music, a full lighting rig with moving heads, wash, effects, lasers, and haze, a DJ booth with CDJs and mixer, and an operator running it all night. We also do extras like CO2 jets, confetti, low fog, and custom gobo projections.

Can we choose our own DJ?

Absolutely. Bring whoever you want. We'll provide the DJ gear and make sure they have everything they need. If you don't have a DJ lined up, we can recommend some good ones.
