FAQ

Battery or mains?

The uplights are battery and fully wireless, so no cables needed. The RGBW pars are mains-powered for bigger outdoor washes where you have power nearby.

Can I set them up myself?

Easily. Set the colour, place them around the room, and you are sorted. We'll show you how on drop-off, or we can set up and run them for you.

How many do I need?

Depends on the room. As a rough guide, one light every couple of metres around the walls looks great. Tell us the venue and we'll suggest a number.
