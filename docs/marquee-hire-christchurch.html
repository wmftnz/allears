Marquee Hire Christchurch

Rain or shine. You're covered.

Canterbury weather does what it wants. If you're running an outdoor event, you need a backup plan. Or better yet, just start with a marquee. We hire out pagodas, popup marquees, and clear roof structures for events of all sizes around Christchurch.

Our marquees go out for weddings on farms, garden parties, corporate functions, festival bars, and VIP areas. We deliver, set up, and pack down. You just turn up and use the space.

Marquee Types

01.

Pagoda Marquees

Clean lines, peaked roof, and they look sharp. Our 5x5m pagodas are great on their own or joined together for larger footprints. Popular for weddings, cocktail areas, and festival bars. They handle wind well and look good in photos.

  • 5x5m footprint
  • Joinable for larger areas
  • Side walls available
02.

Popup Marquees

Quick to set up, easy to move. Our popup marquees are 3x3m and work well for DJ covers, merch areas, or extra shelter at outdoor events. Not as flash as a pagoda, but they get the job done when you need something practical and fast.

  • 3x3m quick deploy
  • Branded walls available
  • Weighted or staked
03.

Clear Roof Marquees

All the shelter with none of the closed-in feeling. Clear roof marquees let the light in during the day and look incredible at night with festoon lights or uplighting inside. A favourite for garden weddings and evening events.

  • Transparent PVC roof
  • Various sizes
  • Lighting add-ons

FAQ

What sizes do you have?

We carry 3x3m popup marquees, 5x5m pagodas, and larger clear roof marquees. Pagodas can be joined together for bigger footprints. Tell us your guest count and the layout you're after, and we'll recommend the right size.

Can you set up on grass or concrete?

Yes to both. On grass we use stakes and guy ropes. On concrete or hard surfaces we use weights and ballast. We'll check the site beforehand if needed.

Do you do marquee lighting too?

Absolutely. Festoon lights, fairy lights, uplighting, and full production lighting can all be rigged inside our marquees. It's one of the most popular add-ons for weddings and evening events.
