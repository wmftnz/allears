Flowdan NZ Tour

Touring production across Aotearoa

Flowdan's NZ tour needed a production that could travel. Different venues, different ceiling heights, different power setups. Same show every night. We handled rigging, lighting, audio, control and FX across multiple cities. The rig was designed to be adaptable. Quick bump-in, consistent output, and a lighting show that hit as hard in a small club as it did in a big room. Touring means solving problems on the fly, and this crew does that well.

Notes from the Show

Touring is a different rhythm to one-off shows. You learn the rig inside out by night two, and by the end of the run the crew is moving like clockwork.
