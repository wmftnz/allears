Vesica Cellar Circle

Designed, built and lit from scratch

The Vesica Cellar Circle was a custom art installation we designed and fabricated for the R&V festival grounds. The brief was simple: build something people want to walk into and stand inside. We took it from concept through to steel fabrication and on-site install. The structure was lit with programmable colour-changing fixtures that shifted through the night, creating a space that felt different every time you passed it. It became one of the most photographed spots on site.

Notes from the Show

This one was a proper build. Weeks of design and fabrication before we even got to Gisborne. On site, the install crew had it standing and lit within a day. It held up across the whole festival with zero issues. We were running the site lighting for R&V at the same time, so it all tied together visually.
