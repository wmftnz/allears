Boogie Ball

All about the dancefloor

The Boogie Ball is exactly what it sounds like. A room full of people who want to dance. The production has to serve that. We brought lighting that kept the energy high, a sound system that filled the room at volume without distortion, and DJ backline so the music never stopped. The lighting design was focused on the dancefloor. Moving heads, colour washes, haze, strobes. Keep it moving, keep it fun, keep it loud.

Notes from the Show

Ball nights are always a good time when the production is right. The room looked great, the sound was clean, and the dancefloor was full all night. That's the goal. We do a stack of these through the season. See the Ensoc Ball for a bigger setup, or the Toolshed Ball for another one.
