Summit Touring - After Dark

Lighting that makes the room

After Dark was a Summit Touring show where the lighting was the whole visual experience. No screens, no video. Just light, haze and energy. We designed and operated the full lighting rig. The approach was atmospheric. Deep colours, slow builds, hard drops. The kind of show where the lighting does the talking and the crowd feels it in their chest before they see it with their eyes. Simple brief, focused execution.

Notes from the Show

When lighting is the only visual element, the operator carries the show. Every cue matters, every transition has weight. This was one of those gigs where less gear, used well, beats more gear used lazily. Clean work from the crew. We do a lot of these lighting-focused gigs for touring promoters.
