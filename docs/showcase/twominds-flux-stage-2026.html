Twominds Flux Stage 2026

Lasers, lights and a whole lot of bass

The Flux Stage is the beating heart of Twominds. It's dark, loud, and completely immersive. For the 2026 edition we pushed the rig further than ever. A full LED screen wall anchored the back of stage, with laser arrays cutting through haze from every angle. The lighting design ran tight with the music, heavy on movement, heavy on colour. FX cannons added punch at the drops. This is the kind of show where every element has to hit hard and stay locked in all night.
