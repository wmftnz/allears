Festoon Lighting Hire Christchurch

Warm lights strung up right. No fuss.

Festoon lighting makes any outdoor space feel like somewhere you want to hang around. Warm white bulbs on black cable, draped between trees or across a marquee. Simple, but it changes the whole mood of a place. We hire festoon for weddings, markets, garden parties, corporate functions, and pretty much anything outdoors that needs a bit of atmosphere after dark.

We handle the lot. We come to your venue, figure out the best way to run the lines, install everything, and come back after to take it down. You don't need to worry about rigging, power runs, or getting up ladders. We bring our own gear and sort it out.

FAQ

How long are the festoon strings?

We have various lengths that we can join together to cover whatever distance you need. Tell us the space you're working with and we'll figure out how much festoon you need and how to run it.

Can you install them on trees and structures?

Yes. We run festoon between trees, along fences, across marquees, between buildings, and off temporary poles. We bring our own rigging gear and cable ties. If there's nothing to attach to, we can set up free-standing poles to run the lines from.

Do you do festoon for weddings?

Absolutely. Weddings are one of the most common uses for our festoon lighting. We come in before the event to install and come back after to remove. It pairs well with our other wedding services like AV hire and speaker hire.
