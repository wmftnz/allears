Staging Hire Christchurch

A proper stage, built to fit your show.

We carry a solid stock of Eurotruss stage decks and can build a stage to whatever size your gig needs. Small riser for a DJ? Sorted. Full main stage for a festival? No worries. Our decks come in 2m x 1m, 2m x 0.5m, 1m x 1m, and 0.5m x 1m sizes, so we can mix and match to get the footprint right. Legs come in all heights, and every deck locks together with clamps so the whole thing is rock solid.

We handle the build and the packdown. Our crew turns up, puts the stage together, levels it, and makes sure it's safe before anyone steps on it. After the show, we come back and pull it all apart. You just tell us where and when.

FAQ

What stage sizes can you build?

Pretty much any size. Our Eurotruss decks come in 2m x 1m, 2m x 0.5m, 1m x 1m, and 0.5m x 1m sizes. We combine them to build whatever footprint you need, from a small DJ riser to a full festival main stage. Height is adjustable with different leg sizes.

Can you do outdoor stages?

Yes. We build outdoor stages all the time for festivals, markets, and outdoor gigs. The legs have adjustable levellers so we can set up on uneven ground. If the surface is soft, we use base plates to spread the load.

Do the stages come with skirting?

Skirting is available as an add-on. Black stage skirt runs around the front and sides to hide the legs and any cables underneath. It gives the stage a cleaner look, especially for corporate events and weddings.
