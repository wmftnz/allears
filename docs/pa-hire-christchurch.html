FAQ

What's the difference between PA hire and speaker hire?

A PA system is the full package: speakers, mixing desk, microphones, stands, and all the cabling. Speaker hire usually means just the speakers and subs themselves. If you need someone to plug in a mic and have it come out the other end, you want PA hire.

Can I hire a PA without an operator?

Yes. We do dry hire for people who are comfortable setting up and running their own sound. We'll deliver the gear, walk you through the setup, and pick it up afterwards. For anything larger or more complex, we'd recommend having one of our operators on site.

How far in advance should I book?

As early as possible. Weekends in summer fill up fast, especially around ball season and festival season. A few weeks notice is usually fine for smaller setups, but for large events we recommend locking it in a couple of months out.
