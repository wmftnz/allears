DJ Backline Hire Christchurch

Industry-standard decks and mixers, ready to go.

We stock the gear that DJs actually want to play on. Our DJ backline hire includes CDJ-3000s, CDJ-3000Xs, and CDJ-2000NXS2s, all running the latest firmware and kept in solid condition. On the mixer side, we carry the DJM-900NXS2 and the DJM-V10. Every unit gets checked and cleaned between hires, so you're not turning up to a booth full of sticky faders and dead hotcues.

You can hire a single CDJ for a studio session, a pair for a small bar gig, or a full 4-deck setup with mixer, booth monitor, and cabling for a festival stage. We also build DJ backline into bigger production packages with sound, lighting, and staging if you need the full show sorted.
